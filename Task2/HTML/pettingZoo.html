The petting zoo!

Our varied animals await to be petted!

Welcome to our Petting Zoo, where fun and learning come together for an unforgettable experience! Perfect for families, school groups, and animal lovers of all ages, our zoo offers a hands-on adventure with a variety of friendly, domesticated animals. Visitors can interact with goats, sheep, rabbits, chickens, and more, creating lasting memories while learning about the care and nature of these charming creatures. Our well-maintained and safe environment ensures a stress-free experience for both visitors and animals, providing the perfect opportunity for children to discover the joys of animal interaction.

Whether you're looking for a fun day out with the family, a unique birthday party venue, or an educational experience for your group, our Petting Zoo has something for everyone. We offer guided tours, animal feeding sessions, and the chance to cuddle and take pictures with your favorite animals.
