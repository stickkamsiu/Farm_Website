A walk about the farm!

A walk around the farm, with a signifier of the beginning and the end!

Explore the entire farm!

Embark on a delightful adventure with our signature farm-to-park walk, the perfect way to explore the best of our farm, petting zoo, play park, and surrounding fields! Starting at the heart of the farm, you'll step into a world of rustic charm and wide open spaces. Take in the fresh country air as you make your way to our friendly petting zoo, where you can interact with a variety of animals, from cuddly bunnies to playful goats. It’s a great way to connect with nature and enjoy the serene surroundings.

As you continue your journey, you’ll head toward our vibrant play park, where kids can burn off some energy on swings, climbing frames, and slides—while adults can relax and enjoy the peaceful scenery. The walk then leads you across beautiful fields, offering stunning views and a chance to embrace the tranquility of the countryside. Finally, you’ll loop back to our farm shop, where you can unwind and treat yourself to some local goodies, farm-fresh produce, or the perfect souvenir to remember your adventure. This scenic, family-friendly walk is an experience that combines nature, animals, and fun—all in one unforgettable journey!
